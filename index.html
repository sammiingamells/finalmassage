LGBT Massage By Flo

My aim is to provide truly holistic massage to LGBT people. I not only acknowledge the roles that mind, body and spirit play in our health but also wider societal power structures. I seek to ease some of the physical symptoms of depression, anxiety and the stresses of surviving late capitalism. My services are • Body positive • Blame and shame free • Consent lead • Gender neutral • Priced by a sliding scale of affordability

What To Expect

Many people I treat have never had a massage before or are nervous and don't know what to expect. That's fine! Here is basic run down of what to expect in and average appointment with me. It's a good idea to arrive at Open Barber's a couple of minutes early to your appointment. There are Sofas you can wait on and you are welcome to help yourself to a hot drink. When I am ready to see you I will come and collect you from the sofas. We will have a short consultation where I will ask you some of the following questions:

  • How are you feeling?
  • Have you had a massage before?
  • What are the main aims of today's treatment?
  • What area's of your body would you like to work on?
  • Do you have any injury's, Illness's, allergies or medical issues that you would like to disclose?

I will then let you know that:

  • We can stop at any time – no questions asked
  • I will always let you know where on your body I am about to start working
  • You are welcome to keep any items of clothing on
  • You will be covered by a sheet the whole time and I will only expose the body part I am currently working on
  • I will check in with you about pressure

Then I will leave the room to let you get changed, onto the couch and under the sheet. I will knock on the door in a couple of minutes and check you are ready. I will turn on some relaxing massage music and commence with the agreed upon treatment. I will stop 5mins before the hour is up and leave the room to allow you to get dressed. Once you are dressed I will come back in for a brief chat to see how you are feeling and to give you aftercare advice. Then you pay me directly (rather than the main till) and the appointment is over!
